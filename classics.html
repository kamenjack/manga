Classic Manga

These are some of my favorite manga, which have also received high ratings on many websites and forums. They’re great choices for anyone who’s just starting to explore manga.

Dragon Ball

The GOAT of shonen manga. Dragon Ball follows the adventures and growth of its protagonist, Son Goku, from childhood to adulthood. Blending adventure, martial arts, friendship, and humor with legendary battle scenes that remain unmatched, Dragon Ball is the perfect place to start if you want to explore shonen manga.

Saint Seiya

Masami Kurumada's Saint Seiya tells the story of mythical warriors defending the goddess Athena. Its combination of epic battles, Greek mythology, and heroism made it a cornerstone of classic shōnen manga.

Candy Candy

Kyoko Mizuki’s Candy Candy follows the life of a young girl growing up amidst love, friendship, and challenges. Its emotional storytelling and romance made it a defining work of shōjo manga.

Rose of Versailles

Riyoko Ikeda's Rose of Versailles is a historical drama set during the French Revolution. With strong heroines and intricate politics, it remains one of the most celebrated shōjo classics.

Akira

Katsuhiro Otomo's Akira is a groundbreaking seinen manga. Set in a post-apocalyptic Tokyo, it explores political intrigue, social unrest, and psychic powers, combining adult themes with stunning artwork.

Golgo 13

Created by Takao Saito, Golgo 13 follows an elite professional assassin. Its realistic storytelling, action, and espionage themes made it a classic seinen series.

Devilman

Go Nagai's Devilman blends horror, action, and philosophical themes. It explores the struggle between good and evil and is widely recognized for its mature storytelling and dark tone.

Rurouni Kenshin

Nobuhiro Watsuki's Rurouni Kenshin combines historical action with compelling character arcs. Its story of redemption, swordsmanship, and adventure has become a beloved classic.
